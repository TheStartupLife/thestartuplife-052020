UX/UI Design

UX Design

User Experience (UX) design is a type of development that deals with the creation of products or services. Their purpose is to solve customers' problems by creating pleasant and efficient experiences. UX design originally came from non-cognitive sciences, with the use of the scientific method when digital industries started taking on the practice to create designs. UX Designers perform countless research that ties in to the clients' goals, missions, and strengths for product creation. After research, designers would start planning and prototyping using tools such as wireframes.

Some of the tasks UX designers typically perform are:

  • Competitor Analysis
  • Product Structure & Startegy
  • Development Planning
  • Corrdination with UI Designers and Developers
  • Tracking Goals & Integration

UI Design

User Interface (UI) Design is a type of development that focuses on the presentation and interaction with the products or services. The sole responsibility of a UI designers relates to the translation of a product. The research that UI designers conduct is about graphic patterns that could be considered onto the product and trends that change over time. Then, they consider the interactive elements of the interface, such as buttons and frames to make them guiding and responsive to users.

Some of the tasks UI designers typically are:

  • User Guides
  • Utilizing responsiveness to elements
  • Creating Interactivity & Animations
  • UI Testing & Protoyping
  • Branding & Graphic Designing

Dependency

Products and services have turned to be a success with both it's functionality and appealing visuals. UX and UI designers normally work with each other to ensure that the designs are as people would want. For instance, the design could look very attractive but can function poorly and slowly without UX. Also, the design could be fully functional and flows with ease, however it could look very ugly without UI. Companies do tend to have separate positions for a better chance of improving the product.
