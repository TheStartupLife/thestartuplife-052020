GetHousing City of Miami Housing Project

People have been struggling with portable housing for a long while. It was also quite a mess as the government was lacking the right workers to be able to improve the situation. It was then that organizations such as Code for America started volunteering to assist with the crisis. The City of Miami has partnered up with technology organizations to solve portable housing. Thus, the GetHousing for the City of Miami Housing Project was created to fulfill the main objective of bringing in more housing.

To begin with, the government had trouble taking care of the housing alone. The engineers that was working for the government were very few and carried the responsibility of handling all of the departments' technical solutions. As a result, the process of coming up with solutions became very slow. There were forms created by the Department of Housing & Community Development in an old website for housing. Elegibility requirements took place in the forms as well. The process had taken about two to four months to be approved after filling them out. That was when the City of Miami needed a guide for quicker methods.

Consecutively, Code for America was one of the proud organizations that wanted to solve the situation. The organization is based on a community of technical experts that each play a different role in the technology field. Their fellowship was created as a type of initiative that would ensure improvement on services. Overall, Code for America wanted to bring in better oportunity for everyone.

In addition to Code of America's skills, the organization was also very committed to grasping the solution. The team went to many departments relating to housing for research purposes. However, the data that they had gathered were just not reliable. A system was soon needed for anyone receiving notifications to keep track of what is going on on their area relating the the matter. UX and UI designers were also involved with the project to ensure the product is as people would interact with ease. Accordingly, the project had started to become a success.

Finally, the City of Miami had decided to issue a partnership to Code for America. Technologists would be sent to the government to work on technological services for six months. Microsoft's Azure Cloud was used to manage databases through the cloud. The City of Miami also funded to manage the costs while achieving the project's goals. Hence, Cooperation was a great necessity in creating the final product.

To conclude, The GetHousing Project continues to shine through. The website and app gethousing.org was modified for people to be able to find portable housing based on preferences inserted inserted into the info field. The new application process halved to between one and two months for approval. Fortunately, The project expanded to other cities such as Savannah and Charlotte. Therefore, The Housing Project still remains active and improves to operate to perform the most optimal experiences for people. It is only up to the people to be supportive and helpful to speed up the modification of the nation.
